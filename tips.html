Tips & Tricks

  1. You can search by either writing an address in the search field or by tapping anywhere on the map.
  2. Double tap for zooming in the map is disabled. You can either "pinch to zoom" or you can activate the zoom controls in settings.
  3. Set your way once you are already on the go, if you stand still for a period of time longer than 5 minutes iOS may terminate the geolocation process without any notice.
  4. You can use Waykup while travelling by bus, train, car, boat or even walking, but it will NOT work underground! Also watch out for connection issues when going through a tunnel.
  5. Keep your phone in a place with good signal, a pocket is a good option.
  6. Save and share your favourite ways. You'll find the buttons to do so in the lower right side of the screen while travelling.
  7. If you love Waykup, please take a minute and rate it in the App Store. It will be much appreciated
