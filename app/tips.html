Tips & Tricks

  • You can search by either writing an address in the search field or by tapping anywhere on the map.
  • Double tap for zooming in the map is disabled. You can either "pinch to zoom" or you can activate the zoom controls in settings.
  • Set your way once you are already on the go, if you stand still for a period of time longer than 5 minutes iOS may terminate the geolocation process without any notice.
  • You can use Waykup while travelling by bus, train, car, boat or even walking, but it will NOT work underground! Also watch out for connection issues when going through a tunnel.
  • Keep your phone in a place with good signal, a pocket is a good option.
  • Save and share your favourite ways. You'll find the buttons to do so in the lower right side of the screen while travelling.
  • If you love Waykup, please take a minute and rate it in the App Store. It will be much appreciated
